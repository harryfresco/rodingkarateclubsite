Our Training & Services

We teach all ages from 4 onwards. Contact us to find out about getting a free lesson and what family discounts we can offer. We also teach in schools as a extra curricular activity. If this is something that is of interest to you please contact us to discuss your requirements.
How To Join

Joining is really easy, you can either just turn up and start straight away.
Or you could just contact us to let us know you are coming. We charge on a pay as you train basis, so there's no real financial commitment. We think that this is only the fair way to pay for Karate lessons.
